Welcome to WDD 130!

This class serves as an introduction to Web design and development. The course is designed to introduce students to basics of Web technology, Web design and development principles. The course uses hands-on activities with students actually participating in simple Web design projects and programming. It is anticipated that students who complete this course will understand the fields of Web design and development and will have a good idea of whether or not they want to pursue Web design and development as a career.
